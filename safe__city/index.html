Our Mission: The Future of Proactive Safety in India

Project Significance: Why SafeCity Matters

SafeCity is not just a map; it's a behavioral change tool. We utilize advanced **Geospatial AI** and real-time data ingestion to predict crime patterns down to the micro-zone level. This means we move from reactive policing to **proactive risk mitigation**. Our platform empowers citizens to make informed decisions about their routes, timing, and travel methods, especially in high-risk areas of Madhya Pradesh.

Technology & Roadmap: The core technology uses time-series analysis on historical FIR (First Information Report) data combined with environmental factors like crowd density and time-of-day traffic flow. The current implementation uses **Firebase Firestore** for live incident feeds and Leaflet for dynamic, satellite-view mapping, providing a powerful, yet lightweight user experience. Our roadmap includes integrating official police data feeds and developing a dedicated SOS mobile application.
